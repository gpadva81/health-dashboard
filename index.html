📋 Key Rules

1. Log everything in Cronometer BEFORE you eat — plan meals, don't just track after
2. Protein first — eat protein portion before carbs/fats
3. No liquid calories except protein shakes
4. Prep chicken in bulk — cook 3-4 lbs Sunday, portion for the week
5. Weigh food — eyeballing = underestimating. Use a food scale.

🚫 Foods to Avoid

Ketchup chips — 750 cal for 150g with barely any protein
Matzo + peanut butter as a meal — low protein density
Any meal under 20g protein
